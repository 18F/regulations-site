Waiting to receive comment tracking number.
Your comment tracking number can help you find and view your public comment later if you want to.

What will happen to your comment

EPA will post it publicly

  • EPA will process your comment before posting it, which may take a few days or weeks depending on the number of comments received.
  • Then EPA will post your comment to the docket folder for this proposed rule (with a few exceptions). You will be able to find your comment there or by searching Regulations.gov for your comment tracking number or keywords from your comment.

EPA will review it & publish a final rule

  • After this comment period ends, EPA will review all comments and prepare a final rule that includes reponses to comments.
  • If you'd like to hear about this final rule, you can subscribe to EPA rulemaking updates.

You can give feedback on this tool

This eRegulations commenting tool was developed by 18F (part of the General Services Administration) in partnership with EPA. The designers and developers would like to hear your thoughts about this tool, including suggestions for improving it. To give feedback open a new issue in this repository (requires a free login).
