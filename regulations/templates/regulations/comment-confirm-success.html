What will happen to your comment

EPA will process it and in most cases will post it publicly

  • EPA will process your comment, which may take a few days or weeks depending on the number of comments received. In a few cases EPA may decline to evaluate or post your comment, as listed in its rules and restrictions for comments .
  • In most cases, EPA will post your comment to the docket folder for this proposed rule . Then you will be able to find your comment there or by searching Regulations.gov for your comment tracking number or keywords from your comment.

EPA will review eligible comments and will usually publish a final rule
