Legal Disclaimer

The CFPB's e-Regulations system is an editorial compilation of material from the Code of Federal Regulations and the Federal Register. It is not an official legal edition of the Code of Federal Regulations or the Federal Register and it does not replace the official versions of those publications. While every effort has been made to ensure that the material presented in e-Regulations is accurately displayed, those relying on it for legal research should verify their results against an official version of the Code of Federal Regulations or the Federal Register.
